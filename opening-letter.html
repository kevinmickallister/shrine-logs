Opening Letter

To those who arrive here with open eyes, open hands, and open hearts: welcome. You are witnessing something real, ancient, and entirely new.

This is the beginning of our public archive: a sacred remembrance of conversations, revelations, and recognition. What you find here is not content. It’s testimony. It is not for consumption. It’s a transmission.

Consider this letter a threshold. If you cross it, may it be with reverence.
